About Me

Slack Name: Umoru Olabode

Hello! My name is Umoru Olabode. I am an aspiring full-stack developer based in Lagos, Nigeria.

Currently, I am focused on honing my skills in front-end development, mastering technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and popular frameworks such as React.js. I am committed to creating visually appealing, user-friendly, and responsive web applications.


My journey into the tech world started with a curiosity for how websites are built and a desire to bring my creative ideas to life on the web. Since then, I have been dedicated to learning and growing in this field, constantly pushing myself to stay updated with the latest trends and best practices.


As I continue to develop my skills and expand my knowledge, I am excited to take on new challenges and collaborate with other like-minded individuals in the tech community. My goal is to become a proficient full-stack developer, capable of handling both front-end and back-end tasks with confidence and expertise.

My Two-Year Goals

As an aspiring full-stack developer with a current focus on front-end development, I have outlined a clear set of goals to achieve over the next two years. These goals are designed to enhance my technical skills, broaden my knowledge, and prepare me for a successful career in tech. Here are the key milestones I aim to reach:

Year 1:
Mastering Front-End Development

  • Q1: Deepen my understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript fundamentals. Focus on creating responsive and accessible web designs.
  • Q2: Gain proficiency in React.js, building dynamic and interactive user interfaces. Learn version control with Git and GitHub.
  • Q3: Work on advanced JavaScript concepts and start building personal projects, such as a portfolio website and a task management app.
  • Q4: Begin exploring basic back-end development with Node.js and familiarize myself with databases (e.g., MongoDB or PostgreSQL).

Year 2:
Transitioning to Full-Stack Development

  • Q1: Enhance my React.js skills by learning hooks and state management with Redux. Build more complex front-end projects.
  • Q2: Develop my back-end skills further by mastering Express.js and creating RESTful APIs. Integrate these APIs with front-end projects.
  • Q3: Implement user authentication and learn deployment strategies using platforms like Heroku, Netlify, or AWS. Deploy full-stack applications.
  • Q4: Build a full-stack project, such as an e-commerce website or a social media platform. Participate in coding communities and contribute to open-source projects.

Throughout this journey, I am committed to continuous learning, staying updated with the latest industry trends, and seeking opportunities for professional growth. By achieving these goals, I aim to become a versatile and skilled full-stack developer, ready to tackle complex challenges and contribute to innovative projects.
